Policy:

Hear4me is a non-profit aiming to help teenagers in need find immediate and long-term mental health support and care. Our website contains a hotline, operated by volunteering psychology post-grad students. Every call will start with the volunteer listening to the teenager and offering mental support, and if necessary redirecting them to a therapist that fits their specific case. Disclaimer: we are not a suicide crisis hotline. In case you or someone you know has any suicidal thoughts or tendencies we recommend calling the national suicidal hotline. 1201. In a scenario in which the parents are influenced by the stigma surrounding mental healthcare, our volunteer will help the teen communicate their needs to their parents, and make sure they get consent for the therapy they want.
